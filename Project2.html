Gryffindor

Gryffindor is one of the four houses at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the Harry Potter series. It values bravery, courage, and chivalry, and its emblematic animal is the lion. Gryffindor's common room is located in Gryffindor Tower , and its head of house is Professor Minerva McGonagall. Famous Gryffindors include Harry Potter, Hermione Granger, and Ron Weasley.

Ravenclaw

Ravenclaw is one of the four houses at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, known for valuing intelligence, creativity, wit, and wisdom. Its emblematic animal is the eagle, symbolizing vision and higher perspective. The house was founded by Rowena Ravenclaw and its colors are blue and silver. Ravenclaw students are often characterized by their love for learning, curiosity, and pursuit of knowledge.

Hufflepuff

Hufflepuff is one of the four houses at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, founded by Helga Hufflepuff. It values qualities such as hard work, loyalty, patience, and fair play. The house emblem is a badger, representing tenacity and perseverance. Hufflepuff's colors are yellow and black, reflecting its warm and welcoming nature.

Slytherin

Slytherin is one of the four houses at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, founded by Salazar Slytherin. It values ambition, cunning, resourcefulness, and determination. The house emblem is a serpent, symbolizing cleverness and adaptability. Slytherin's colors are green and silver, reflecting its traits of ambition and leadership.
